Stress Detection

    INTRODUCTION

    Stress is a mental or emotional state brought on by demanding or unavoidable circumstances, also referred to as stressors. In order to prevent any unfavorable occurrences in life, it is crucial to understand human stress levels. Sleep disturbances are related to a number of physical, mental, and social problems.

    Work, relationships, money, and health issues are common sources of stress. Body's Response: During stress, our body releases hormones (like cortisol and adrenaline) to prepare us to act, increasing heart rate and energy.

    Short-term stress helps us handle immediate challenges. Long-term stress can harm health, leading to anxiety, depression, heart problems, weak immunity, and trouble thinking clearly. Managing stress helps keep our mind and body healthy. Identifying what causes stress and learning to cope builds resilience

    This Machine learning model detects human Stress based on sleep-related behaviors aiming to Spot stress early. Give people insights to manage stress better.
